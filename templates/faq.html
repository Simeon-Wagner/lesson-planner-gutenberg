LLM hallucination

Large-Language-Models (LLM) suffer from a phenomenon called "hallucination". If it does not have sufficient facts, a LLM will not be aware of it and will just try to give you a text that seems formally correct. Therefore, LLMs even make up facts that are not true. Therefore, you should always check the result for plausibility and not ask questions to LLM where you already expect it to have no or only few information about.
